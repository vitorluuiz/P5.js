Circles Box is a thrilling web game where players navigate a sphere across the screen using their mouse, aiming to avoid collisions with other moving spheres. The objective is simple: stay alive for as long as possible amidst dynamic shifts and collisions, creating an intense gaming experience.

Control your sphere with quick reflexes and strategic maneuvers to evade obstacles. One wrong move, and it's game over. Your score is based on survival time, encouraging players to beat their own records.

But the excitement doesn't end there. Circles Box offers a multiplayer mode for interactive experiences. Join existing rooms or create your own, engaging in real-time battles with friends or strangers worldwide.

To enhance immersion, the game provides sound options and adjustable settings like sphere speed and quantity, allowing players to customize their experience and tailor difficulty to their liking.
